About Hotel California Aruba

Hotel California Aruba 3-star ⭐⭐⭐ Long Term Bed and Breakfast located in the main street, downtown Oranjestad the capital of Aruba, a Dutch island in the Caribbean Sea. An affordable hotel perfectly situated to visit many tourist attractions of the city within walking distance. Our combination of affordable rooms, amenities and outstanding customer service makes our hotel the best accommodation for your vacation.

What's nearby the hotel

The hotel's central location makes it easy to access public transportation and business destinations. Discover the rich culture of Aruba, where the colorful heritage, culinary styles and musical traditions infuse every aspect of daily life.

Arubian society is multilingual, most of the population speaks more than four languages (Dutch, Spanish, English & Papiamento). The weather is usually sunny and dry, it is always summer in Aruba, with slight variations in temperature in the year, and very sporadic rainfall with an average of 28°C.

Means of payment

You can choose the most convenient payment option during the booking. You can pay for your accommodation either online by credit or debit card, or upon arrival with confirmed bookings. In case of booking a non-refundable room rate, the total reservation will be charged and is non-refundable or transferable. Please contact our staff to get more information about this method of payment.

Cancellation policy

Upon cancellation of a reservation, the customer will be charged a certain percentage of the total price of accommodation. If canceled up to 48 hours before the date of arrival, no fee will be charged.

Check-in & check-out

Check-in starts at 16:00. Should you arrive earlier than that, we will do our best to get your room ready as quickly as possible. However, we cannot guarantee that we will have a room ready before that time. If you do need to wait to check in, feel free to store your suitcases in our luggage room. You can check out until noon. If you’d like to stay in your room longer, please coordinate with the reception.
