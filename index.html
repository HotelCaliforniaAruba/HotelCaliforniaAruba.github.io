About Hotel California Aruba

Hotel California Aruba 3-star ⭐⭐⭐ Long Term Bed and Breakfast located in the main street, downtown Oranjestad the capital of Aruba, a Dutch island in the Caribbean Sea. An affordable hotel perfectly situated to visit many tourist attractions of the city within walking distance. Our combination of affordable rooms, amenities and outstanding customer service makes our hotel the best accommodation for your vacation.

What's nearby the hotel

The hotel's central location makes it easy to access public transportation and business destinations. Discover the rich culture of Aruba, where the colorful heritage, culinary styles and musical traditions infuse every aspect of daily life. Arubian society is multilingual, most of the population speaks more than four languages (Dutch, Spanish, English & Papiamento). The weather is usually sunny and dry, it is always summer in Aruba, with slight variations in temperature in the year, and very sporadic rainfall with an average of 28°C.
